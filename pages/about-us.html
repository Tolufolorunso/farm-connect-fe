A Bit About Us

FarmConnect is an Innovative Agriculture Platform that empowers farmers by providing a link to investors, helping them with improved seeds, farm inputs, training on modern farming techniques and mentorship program. This gives the farmers the capacity and funds to better farming equipmens and sustainable funding which leads to increased food production and security in Africa.
